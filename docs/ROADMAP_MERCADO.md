# Roadmap — Streamer Scheduler

**A simple tool to plan your streams, promote them automatically, and grow your audience.**

*Last updated: March 2026*

---

## What you can do today

Streamer Scheduler helps creators stay consistent and promote their streams without extra work.

**Current features:**

- **Stream scheduling calendar** — Plan streams and content with a simple visual calendar.
- **Multi-platform publishing** — Schedule announcements across Twitch, Discord, X, Instagram and YouTube.
- **Creator integrations** — Login and connect accounts using Twitch, Google, Discord or X.
- **Flexible plans** — Free trial available with simple Starter and Pro plans.
- **Multi-language support** — Available in English and Spanish.

---

## Problems we solve

Streaming consistently is harder than it should be.

Creators often struggle with:

- **Promoting their streams** — Many streamers forget to announce their streams before going live.
- **Managing multiple platforms** — Posting on Twitch, Discord, Twitter and other platforms takes time.
- **Keeping their community informed** — Fans don’t always know when the next stream starts.
- **Maintaining a consistent schedule** — Without planning, growth becomes unpredictable.

Streamer Scheduler helps solve these problems with simple automation.

## What we are building next

### Auto-Promotion Engine

One of the most requested features.

When you schedule a stream, the platform will automatically promote it across your connected platforms.

**Example workflow:**

- 24h before stream → announcement post  
- 3h before stream → reminder  
- 30 min before → last call  
- Going live → automatic notification  

*Schedule once. Promotion happens automatically.*

### Public Streamer Pages

Each creator will have a public page (e.g. *streamerscheduler.com/username*).

Fans will be able to see:

- Upcoming streams  
- Countdown timer  
- Creator links  
- Schedule overview  

Useful for bios, communities and discoverability.

### Notifications

More ways to keep creators informed:

- Email reminders  
- Discord alerts  
- Push notifications (PWA)  

### Stream Analytics

Designed for small creators, not enterprise teams.

Insights will include:

- Best streaming days  
- Most consistent schedules  
- Stream history  
- Growth patterns  

### Planned integrations

More creator platforms are planned. Potential integrations include:

- **Kick**
- **TikTok**
- **YouTube Live**
- **OBS plugins and extensions**

The order may change based on community feedback.
